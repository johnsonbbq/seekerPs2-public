# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

SeekerPS2 ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SeekerPS2 handles information when you use the application or review the public project repository.

## Overview

SeekerPS2 is designed to operate primarily on the user's device. This project does not require account registration and is not intended to operate a central user-data service for normal app use.

## Information the App May Process

Depending on how the app is used, SeekerPS2 may process or store limited information such as:

- BIOS directories selected by the user
- Game library paths selected by the user
- Save files, save states, screenshots, and related local files
- App settings and preferences stored on the device
- Basic device or runtime information needed for app functionality, rendering, input, compatibility, or stability

## Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

This project is not intended to collect personally identifiable information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Exact location
- Payment card information
- Government-issued identification

## Local Storage

Most app data is intended to remain on the user's device. Users choose the storage locations used for BIOS files, games, saves, screenshots, and related content.

## Network Access and Third-Party Services

Some optional app features may contact third-party services, public repositories, or external content sources, depending on user actions and app configuration.

When this happens:
- Requests may be sent directly from the user device to those third-party services
- Those services operate under their own privacy policies and terms
- SeekerPS2 does not guarantee the privacy practices of external platforms or content providers

## How Information May Be Used

Any information processed by the app may be used only for purposes such as:

- Loading and organizing local content
- Saving settings and user-generated files
- Supporting compatibility, rendering, input, and stability
- Diagnosing app-level issues experienced by the user

## Data Sharing

This project is not intended to sell personal information or operate a central profile database for users.

## Data Security

Because SeekerPS2 is designed to work primarily with local device storage, users are responsible for securing their own devices, backups, and selected file locations.

## Children's Privacy

SeekerPS2 is not specifically directed toward children under 13, and this project does not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions may be published in the project repository.
